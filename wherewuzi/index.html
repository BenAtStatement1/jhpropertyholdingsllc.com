What is whereWUZi?

whereWUZi is a Windows app that quietly logs where you've been — by date, city, and network — automatically in the background. Once installed, it runs silently and builds a dated record of your location history without any interaction required.

Your location log is stored only on your device. Nothing is uploaded, synced, or shared. whereWUZi never asks for an account and never connects to any cloud service.

Features

📍

Daily Location Logging

Automatically logs your approximate location once per day using your public IP address. No GPS required.

🔕

Runs in the Background

Logs silently while you work. No popups, no interruptions. Open the app anytime to review your history.

🔒

Stays on Your Device

Your location history is stored locally in a plain text file. No account, no cloud, no uploads — ever.

🎯

Precision Location ★ Premium

Uses Windows location services (Wi-Fi or GPS) for street-level accuracy with a full address in each log entry.

🌐

Network Change Logging ★ Premium

Logs an additional entry whenever your device connects to a new network with a different public IP address.

📋

Simple Log Viewer

Browse, search, and sort your location history by date, city, or region. Export or copy entries anytime.

Who uses whereWUZi?

whereWUZi is built for people who need a reliable, private record of where they've been:

  • Business travelers — document travel dates and locations for expense reports and reimbursement.
  • Credit card review — quickly verify that charges match where you actually were on a given date.
  • Domicile and residency documentation — establish a dated record of physical presence for tax residency, state domicile, or legal purposes.
  • Frequent movers — people who split time between homes, cities, or states and need to track where they spent time.
  • Remote workers — document work locations for compliance or reimbursement purposes.
  • Anyone who wants a private location history — without giving it to Google, Apple, or any other service.

Privacy first

whereWUZi was built on a simple principle: your location history belongs to you. The app uses ipinfo.io to look up your approximate location from your public IP address — that is the only outbound connection the app makes. Your location log never leaves your device.
